Core Modules
Deviation Management
Capture, investigate, and close - end to end
  • Structured intake with area, product, and batch classification
  • Tabbed investigation workflow: root cause, impact, and QA acceptance
  • Automatic overdue flagging with escalation indicators
  • Seamless link to CAPA records from within each deviation
CAPA & Efficacy Reviews
From action plan to verified effectiveness
  • Role-gated 6-stage workflow: Draft → Approved → Implemented → Verified
  • Electronic signatures with timestamp for every state transition
  • Built-in Efficacy Review module to confirm CAPA effectiveness
  • Full deviation linkage - trace every action back to its source
Change Control
Multi-tier approval for controlled changes
  • Structured 3-tier review chain: HR → Subject Matter Expert → SC
  • Each reviewer has an independent approve / reject / abstain action
  • Automatic status progression with full decision audit trail
  • Change category and risk impact captured at initiation
Dashboard & Analytics
Live quality intelligence at a glance
  • Executive KPI cards: open deviations, overdue CAPAs, CCs in review
  • Deviation trend chart and area-breakdown bar chart
  • Quality Analytics with compliance rates and resolution heatmaps
  • Report generator with printable quality summaries
